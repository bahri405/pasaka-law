Area of Practices

Through our premium, efficient and cost effective legal services, the independent law professionals at Sandiva Legal Network are dedicated to provide flexible support and resources our clients need to accomplish their business goals and objectives.

Business Establishment

Our lawyers have assisted several clients on their establishment of the company. Not only for a simple start-up company, but also suc-ceeded in assisting a cooperation of the parties in establishing the cooperation and joint venture company. We assisted our clients in preparing the best structure of cooperation for their business establishment. Our commitment is to ensure the parties understand their commitment respectively.

Commercial

Our experience to assist our clients in closing the deal was success-fully made due to our capability to negotiate for the interest of our clients. In fact, our involvement in several deals required us to be the negotiator and stood in the middle to also assist the counter-part for their non-understanding on the legal matters. Our position in this negotiation simply to close this deal effectively and efficient-ly for the clients’ interest. We apply the principle that the client is us, and we see such deal as our own deal, which make the successful-ness of the clients to close the deal is also our success

Banking and Finance

Our network with finance institution and experience with the banking have given our firm leverage to understand not only the laws but also very familiar with the detail of documents/stages needed in the bank. Our network has given us a plus point which our client can gain benefit from it. We have experienced assisting the creditor and debtor, whether it is personally or companies. We know how the bank is very strict with their standard of procedure and documentation, which this knowledge has resulted us to know on how to support our client in urgency.

Corporate & Compliance

Our lawyers are able to see the company from outside and inside. Our experience in closing the deal or made the legal arrangement signed by the clients is an advantageous point, however our experience to peel the company in order to see if it has complied with the compliance is another plus point. Our experience in assisting the clients in the transaction documents has made us as strong corporate law firm, meanwhile our experience in analysing, assessing, auditing, and examining the clients’ companies is another strong point to see the compliance of our clients. Our experience in the capital market and acquisition transaction has given us the ability to see the company from the inside. We have supported the corporate and compliance of multi sector business and companies

Real Estate & Property

Our lawyers have very strong knowledge and experience in the property sector. We have assisted our clients from acquisition of property, property lease, running the property business such as building operator or manager and hotel and resort. For the land acquisition, we have assisted our clients to negotiate with the local landowner, including guided the local landowner to prepare all necessary documents for the completion of the transaction. Our assistance for the counterpart (landowners) usually is needed for the uncertified lands and family issues.

Mergers and Acquisitions

We have experienced and assisted our clients in the relevant trans action, from property sector, logistics sector, and general compa nies. We have experienced in performing due diligence and prepar ing legal documents. Our firm also have network with other support ing professional for the relevant transaction, and we are able to work together with finance and tax consultant.
